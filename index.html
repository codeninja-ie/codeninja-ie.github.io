< Code Ninja >

The student app / game development competition

Register Now

CodeNinja is a student app development competition. It's designed to train and encourage students to be creative in the cultivation of their own tech ideas. It is open to both individuals and groups that want to build web and mobile applications.



Judging Criteria


  • Quality

    Is your App functional? How good is your coding style, modularity, documentation and overall architecture?

  • Novelty Value

    How unique is your App when compared to other offerings? Does it stand out from and improve upon existing competitors?

  • Usability

    Can you demonstrate a superior user experience? Is it tailored to your target audience?

  • Market Potential

    Can you build an App that customers will buy? Do you have a strategy for growing your customer base?



Rules

  1. The competition is open to students (under- and postgraduate) of NUI Galway and GMIT
  2. Closing date for entries is 1pm on Monday, March 12th, 2018. You should Register first, to let us know that you intend to submit an entry.
  3. Submissions can be either a mobile, desktop or web application or game.
  4. You can enter as either a team or an individual
  5. Strictly no plagiarism allowed. Your submission must be your own work.
    • Using frameworks, libraries, templates or scaffold projects is of course allowed.
    • Where using open source code, make sure to preserve any licenses and give credit where it is due.
  6. Use version control to show your work and progress.
